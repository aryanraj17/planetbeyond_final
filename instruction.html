Exoplanet Gravity Challenge: Instructions

Welcome to Exoplanet Gravity Challenge! In this game, you will navigate your character across different exoplanets while avoiding falling objects influenced by varying gravitational forces. Follow the instructions below to get started!

Objective

The goal of the game is to avoid falling objects as you navigate through different exoplanets. Each planet has a unique gravitational pull that affects how the objects fall. Survive as long as possible to increase your score!

Controls

  • Move Right: Press the Right Arrow Key on your keyboard.
  • Move Left: Press the Left Arrow Key on your keyboard.
  • Restart Game: If you collide with an object, press the R Key to restart the game.
